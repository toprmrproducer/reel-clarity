Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Your files never leave your device

ReelClarity processes video entirely client-side, in your browser. When you choose a file, it is read locally and enhanced using JavaScript running on your own device. At no point is the video file uploaded, transmitted, or sent to any server we operate. We have no way to see, store, or access the videos you process.

What we don't collect

We do not require an account, we do not ask for your email, and we do not store your files. Nothing about the content of your video (its name, size, or pixels) is transmitted anywhere.

Standard web analytics

Like most websites, basic, aggregated analytics (such as page views) and advertising may be served on this site through third-party providers. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies consistent with their own privacy policies. This is separate from, and unrelated to, any video file you process with the tool above.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to the RapidXAI team viarapidxai.com.

Every enhancement runs locally in your browser. Nothing you upload is ever sent to a server.
