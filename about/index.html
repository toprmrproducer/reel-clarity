About ReelClarity

ReelClarity does one thing: it enhances the resolution and sharpness of a video, entirely inside your browser. No accounts, no uploads to a server, no waiting in a queue.

We built it as a single-purpose tool on purpose. Instead of bolting video enhancement onto a crowded do-everything utility site, ReelClarity exists to do this one job well — quickly, privately, and without clutter.

Under the hood, ReelClarity tries to run a real on-device AI super-resolution model (ESRGAN) frame by frame. If your device can't keep up, it automatically falls back to a fast, high-quality resample-and-sharpen pass so you still walk away with a usable, enhanced file. Either way, everything happens locally — your video never leaves your machine.

ReelClarity is part of a small family of focused, single-purpose browser tools built by RapidXAI.

Every enhancement runs locally in your browser. Nothing you upload is ever sent to a server.
